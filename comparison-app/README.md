# Comparison Problem Builder

This is a new app for first graders to learn comparison problems.

## How it works

1. Type a simple comparison word problem into the text area.
2. Click the **Answer** button.
3. The app shows the problem and reveals:
   - a big rectangle first
   - a small rectangle after 12 seconds
   - a comparison circle after another 12 seconds
4. The numbers in the problem are highlighted and placed into the correct shapes.
5. Click the `?` in the unknown shape to reveal the answer as an equation.

## Example

Problem: `Jack has 5 apples. Jill has 10 more apples. How many apples does Jill have?`

- Big rectangle: `?`
- Small rectangle: `5`
- Circle: `10`
- Click `?` to show: `10 + 5 = 15`
